Vito Laterza is an anthropologist, media scholar, and political analyst. He is Associate Professor of Global Development and Planning at the University of Agder, Norway. A Cambridge PhD, his current work focuses on digital media, political communication, Big Tech platforms, and the dialectic between [analogue and digital humanity](https://www.academia.edu/attachments/105041267/download_file?s=portfolio).
