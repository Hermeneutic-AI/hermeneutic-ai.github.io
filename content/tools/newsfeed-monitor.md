Newsfeed Monitor watches streams of news coverage the way a fieldworker watches a site: attentively, over time, with an eye for framing, recurrence, and change.

<!--more-->

For researchers who study public discourse — media sociologists, political ethnographers, discourse analysts — it tracks how stories are told and retold across outlets, how vocabularies shift, and how events are made meaningful. The output is not a feed of links but an interpretive log: a running account of framings and themes that a researcher can interrogate, correct, and build on.

**Status:** in active development.
