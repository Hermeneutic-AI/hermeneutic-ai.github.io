Book Parlor is a planned space for slow reading in the company of machines: a setting where a scholar and an AI reader sit with a book together.

<!--more-->

The idea is conversational rather than extractive — not "summarize this monograph," but the kind of talk that happens in a good seminar: What is this chapter really doing? Where does the argument strain? What would Bourdieu, or Arendt, or the author's own earlier self, say back?

**Status:** planned. Book Parlor is further out on our horizon; notes on its design will appear here as the thinking develops.
