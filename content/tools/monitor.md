Monitor is the lab's live reading room — a running dashboard that follows several streams at once: developments in artificial intelligence, in space, and across the left and right of political discourse, watching how each is framed and reframed over time.

<!--more-->

Use the tabs within the panel below to move between feeds. Each is meant as an interpretive log rather than a raw wire — an attempt to read the news the way a fieldworker reads a site: attentively, and over time.
