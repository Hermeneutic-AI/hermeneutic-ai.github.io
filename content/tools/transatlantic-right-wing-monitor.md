Transatlantic Right-Wing Monitor is a standalone dashboard following right-wing and far-right politics across more than ten countries on both sides of the Atlantic — parties, leading figures, movements, and transnational networks, tracked country by country.

<!--more-->

Where the News Monitor's Right feed offers a single running stream, this tool takes the comparative view: the same watchful attention applied across national contexts at once, so that parallel developments — and the connections between them — become visible. It is built as a research instrument: analytical observation of the transatlantic right, sustained over time.
