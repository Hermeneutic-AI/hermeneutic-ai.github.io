Hermeneutic AI is a social science research lab studying **interpretive artificial intelligence** — how large language models and related systems can serve as instruments of interpretation, and what they mean for the craft of understanding.

We build tools and write research notes for sociologists, anthropologists, and scholars across the interpretive social sciences and humanities and for those who are interested in these disciplines: researchers and analysts whose work turns on meaning, context, and close reading rather than prediction alone.
